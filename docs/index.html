We made a cyberpunk short animation using the CG software maya and blender. It was the first time for all of us to use Maya, and none of us had any 3DCG experience. However, if we didn't know something, we looked it up again and again and tried various methods to make it through a trial-and-error process. Also, we had to make their own CG models of each items and then place them in the same scene. It was so difficult to harmonize them because the colors, styles ware so different without matching. But all those hard experiences became a great 3DCG lesson for us. We hope you enjoy the cyberpunk world we created .

This is the art team, our team strives to make something that was crafted with passion and care. With inspiration and lots of time, anything could be art. While the team had trouble with figuring out each individual's art piece, we managed and completed these pieces of art. We are proud of what we made and hope that this will inspire others to create with confidence and pride.

We made MV by combining of real pictures and paintings. Speaking of visual works, we make importance to mix images of real two dimension. By combining them, we can express the taste of strange and horror little bit, added to Cyber Punk style. That’s what we would like to express. Please enjoy it.

We are the video team! We made a behind-the-scenes video. We were not having too much experience about this, but we did our best on this! We hope you can see how our group worked this all out!
